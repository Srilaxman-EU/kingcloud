⚠️ CORS Configuration Required

For this app to access your Storj bucket from the browser, you must configure CORS on your bucket. Add the following CORS rule in your Storj bucket settings: 

[
  {
    "AllowedOrigins": ["*"],
    "AllowedMethods": ["GET", "PUT", "DELETE", "HEAD"],
    "AllowedHeaders": ["*"],
    "ExposeHeaders":  ["ETag", "Content-Length", "Content-Type"],
    "MaxAgeSeconds":  3600
  }
]

For production, replace "*" in AllowedOrigins with your specific domain.
